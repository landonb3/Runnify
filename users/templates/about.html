Meet Runnify, your new running partner



Features

Curated Spotify playlists that can be downloaded to a user's account

Over 120 music genres to choose from

Dashboard with playlists history and analytics

Option for stride length estimation or a provided tempo



How does Runnify work?

First, provide Runnify with your preferred music genre, run length, pace, and either a stride length, height, or tempo

Using Spotify's Web API and our own tempo calculating algorithms, Runnify will return a playlist of songs that match your tempo

Lastly, after connecting your Spotify account to Runnify, you can add the playlist to your Spotify library, and have it available for playback on your desktop, laptop, or mobile device
