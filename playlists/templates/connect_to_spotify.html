Connect to Spotify


Step One

Ensure your Runnify and Spotify username match. See here if you have troubles finding your Spotify username


Step Two

Click below to sign into Spotify

After logging in, allow Runnify to have access to your public data and to create private playlists




Step Three

Send a Runnify Playlist to Spotify by selecting a playlist and clicking on "Send to Spotify"



For your first playlist, Spotify will redirect you to Runnify's home screen. Copy the redirect url and paste it into the command line.



That's it! Your Runnify account is now connected with Spotify.
